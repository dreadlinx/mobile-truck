About Mobile Truck Clinic

Your trusted partner for mobile truck repairs and spare parts

Who We Are

Mobile Truck Clinic is a proudly South African company established in 2016. We specialize in heavy-duty truck repairs, engine rebuilds, and supplying top-quality spare parts for trucks, buses, and LDVs.

Our experienced technician, Phillimon Shoniwa, leads a team committed to fast, affordable, and mobile truck repair solutions. Whether you're stuck on the road or need workshop-level service, we're ready to assist.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive solutions for all your truck maintenance needs:

  • Engine Rebuild & Maintenance
  • Gearbox & Differential Rebuilds
  • Truck & Bus Spare Parts
  • Servicing LDVs and Bakkies
  • 24/7 Emergency Roadside Assistance
